V1 Original — selected and rolled out

Max picked V1. The hexagonal emblem is now wired across the site — header on every page, favicon, app icon and home-page hero. The other variants below are kept on this page for reference in case the direction changes later.

How these were made

The Crystal Brook AI Image Builder generated each emblem from scratch — same engine that draws your fish products, just pointed at logo emblem prompts in vintage steel-engraving style. Each variant uses the same heritage gold-on-black aesthetic; only the frame shape changes (hexagon, oval, roundel, shield).

If you like a direction but want a tweak — different pose for the bass, slightly different frame, your name added — just say so. Each one takes ~5 seconds to re-roll.

Note on text: AI image generators sometimes mis-spell small or curved text. The look-and-feel is what matters here — once you pick a direction, we'll lock the wordmark and re-roll until every letter is perfect.

03

Oval Crest

Classic apothecary / heritage badge shape with ornate scrollwork.

Most ornate
V3 · Oval Crest · gold on warm black

What it does well

  • Most "premium artisan" feel of all the variants
  • Decorative top + bottom flourishes feel hand-engraved
  • Vertical orientation works well on tall packaging labels
  • "CRYSTAL BROOK" curving along the top is a signature mark

What it gives up

  • Vertical shape means less wide on the website header
  • Ornate flourishes won't render at favicon size

Best used for

Packaging labels (tall + vertical), letterhead, business card front, the "premium product" stamp on each mount.

04

Circular Roundel

Built specifically for circles — favicon, Instagram avatar, app icon.

Avatar-ready
V4 · Circular Roundel · gold on warm black

What it does well

  • Owns the Instagram avatar slot (where most people first see you)
  • Curved type around the bass = unmistakable "stamp" feel
  • Reads as a single mark from across a room
  • Looks great branded onto product packaging stickers

What it gives up

  • Curved type is hard to read at very small sizes
  • Better paired with a wordmark for header use

Best used for

Favicon, app icon, social avatars (Insta / Facebook / TikTok), wax-seal stickers on packaging, back-of-mount stamp.

05

Heraldic Shield

Most "premium brand" of all — full heraldic crest with ornate scrollwork.

Most prestigious
V5 · Heraldic Shield · gold on warm black

What it does well

  • The most "premium heritage brand" of all five
  • Bigger frame = more room for the bass illustration
  • Top banner ribbon + bottom motto = real coat-of-arms feel
  • Stands out next to other Aussie outdoor brands (Filson, RM Williams)

What it gives up

  • Heaviest visual weight — competes with product photography
  • Ornate scrollwork won't survive at favicon size
  • Some buyers may find it "too formal" for an artisan brand

Best used for

Premium product certificate, framed wall display in your workshop, top of the website on the "About Max" page, premium edition packaging.
