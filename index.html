The Craft

Built by hand,
made to outlast us.

Every piece starts as an archival-grade print, hand-cut to shape, mounted on Australian hardwood, then sealed under jewellery-grade epoxy. No mass production — every mount is hand-finished, signed and numbered. Imperfections aren't hidden; they're the fingerprint that tells you it's real.

  • 01 Archival print selected and sized to the piece
  • 02 Image hand-cut to shape and trimmed for depth
  • 03 Mounted onto Australian hardwood backing
  • 04 Resin poured in stages, set for 48 hours, signed & numbered
