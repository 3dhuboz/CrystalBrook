The headline feature

Image Builder.

Need a new product photo? You don't need a camera, a lightbox, or three hours in front of an AI prompt editor. You type. It draws. You pick.

How it works

Plain English in. Print-ready PNG out.

Three steps. No fancy "AI prompt" knowledge needed. The system speaks fishing, cars, dogs, birds and Australian wildlife — including FNQ-specific species. If you say "Cairns butterfly" it knows you mean the Birdwing.

STEP 1

Pick a kind

🐠 Saltwater · 🎣 Freshwater · 🚗 Classic car · 🐾 Animal · 🦜 Bird · ✨ Anything else

→
STEP 2

Describe it

"a barra leaping out of the water" or "a 1971 Mach-1 Mustang in side profile"

→
STEP 3

Pick your favourite

4 photos appear in 10 seconds. Use one in the shop. Download the print version.

Under the hood: your description goes to Llama 3.1 8B Instruct which expands it into a detailed prompt with proper species names, colours and pose. That feeds FLUX 1 Schnell which renders 4 photos in parallel, all isolated on white background, ready to drop straight into the catalogue.

AI

Edge

Stateless serverless functions running on every continent. Cold start <5ms. Same network as your storefront.

PWA

Installable, offline-ready

Service worker pre-caches the shell. Customers can add Crystal Brook to their home screen — opens like a native app, works on the train.

Vision

Client-side background removal

Custom flood-fill algorithm in the customer wizard. White edges become transparent in the browser, no server round-trip.

Pay
