The fine print, in plain English

Shipping & Returns.

No legalese. No surprises. Here's exactly how Max ships your piece, and what happens if anything goes sideways.

Shipping → Returns → Workmanship guarantee → Custom commissions →
01

Shipping

Australia-wide. Free, always.

Free Australia-wide shipping

Every order ships free to your door, anywhere in Australia. No threshold, no surcharge for the far corners — same delivery to Cairns or Hobart, on us.

Production: 4–6 weeks

Each piece is made to order in the Gordonvale workshop. Print, cut, mount, three resin pours, 48-hour cure, signed and numbered. We don't rush the resin.

Transit: 3–5 business days

Once Max packs it (in a custom timber crate for anything over 80 cm), it ships via Australia Post tracked. Tracking link emailed the moment it leaves the workshop.

International? Drop us a line

We've shipped to NZ, Singapore and the UK. Ask for a quote at mandpwhite@optusnet.com.au with your suburb and the piece you're after.

Big pieces. Anything over 100 × 70 cm goes road freight (still tracked, still insured) — allow an extra few days, and we'll co-ordinate a delivery window with you. Lift gate available on request.
02

Returns

If it arrives wrong, we make it right.

Damaged in transit

Send a photo within 72 hours of delivery and we'll replace the piece at no cost — Max keeps the broken one for parts. Couriers happen. We've got it covered.

Wrong piece sent

If we ship the wrong fish (or wrong timber, or wrong size), we cover return postage and rush a corrected piece to the front of the queue.

Changed your mind

Standard catalogue pieces (not custom commissions): you can return within 14 days of delivery. Piece must be unhung and in original packaging. Return postage on you, and a 15% restocking fee covers our shipping cost out + back. The rest is refunded to your original payment within 5 business days.

Custom commissions

Once Max has printed your one-off, it's yours. Deposits are fully refundable up to print stage (usually 5 business days from order). After that, we'll work with you on revisions but can't refund the print + timber cost.

03

Workmanship guarantee

If the resin yellows or the timber cracks — we'll make it right.

Every Crystal Brook piece is built to outlive its owner. We use UV-stabilised, jewellery-grade two-part epoxy and Australian hardwoods that have been seasoned for years. If anything fails through manufacturing, you've got Max's word in writing.

  • Resin yellowing Lifetime cover. Send a photo, we'll re-pour or rebuild it. Just keep it out of direct equatorial sun for 6+ hours daily.
  • Print fading Archival pigment ink rated 100+ years indoors. If it fades inside that window from anything other than direct sun, we'll reprint and remount.
  • Timber cracking / warping 5-year cover under normal indoor conditions (heated/cooled rooms, not bathrooms). After 5 years, rebuild at cost.
  • Mounting hardware failure Lifetime — heavy-duty D-rings rated 4× the piece weight come standard.
04

Custom commissions

Your photo on the wall.

A few extra notes for one-of-a-kind pieces:

  • Photo quality matters Higher resolution = better print. We'll let you know within 24 hrs if your photo isn't workable, and refund any deposit. Phone snaps are usually fine — old prints, not so much.
  • Approval before pour Max emails a digital mockup before he prints. You sign off, then production begins. No surprises.
  • Lead time 5–7 weeks for commissions (one extra week for the back-and-forth). Faster turnaround can be arranged for an extra fee.
  • Copyright You confirm you own or have permission to print the photo. Crystal Brook keeps no rights to the image.

Still got questions?

Easiest way is a phone call to (07) 4056 1887 or an email to mandpwhite@optusnet.com.au. Max usually replies same-day.
